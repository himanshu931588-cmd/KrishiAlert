# KrishiAlert
KishanSeva is a unifie digital platform designed to eliminate post-harvest stubble burning across North India. By bridging the gap between high-tech satellite monitoring and on-ground resource accessibility, we provide farmers with a viable, profitable alternative to crop residue burning.
🌾 The Challenge
Every year, a critical 15-day harvest window forces farmers into a "burn or lose" dilemma. Expensive Crop Residue Management (CRM) machinery—like Happy Seeders and Balers—remains out of reach due to:

High Rental Costs: Prohibitive pricing for individual small-scale farmers.

Logistical Gaps: Inability to locate available machinery or labor in real-time.

Delayed Incentives: Governmental subsidies often fail to reach the farmer before the burning window closes.

🚀 Our Solution
KishanSeva transforms a seasonal crisis into a managed logistical operation through three core pillars:

🛰️ Predictive Hotspot Analysis
We integrate NASA Satellite API data with machine learning models to identify high-risk burning zones in real-time. By analyzing soil moisture, harvest patterns, and historical data, we predict where fires are likely to start before they are lit.

🚜 Resource "Bazaar" (Marketplace)
A hyper-local, peer-to-peer marketplace that allows farmers to:

Rent CRM Machinery: Instantly locate and book Happy Seeders, Mulchers, and Balers.

Secure Labor: Connect with verified labor pools specialized in field clearing.

Track Availability: Real-time GPS tracking of equipment to ensure timely arrival within the 15-day window.

💸 Instant Subsidy Routing
Our fintech layer automates the verification process, routing government subsidies and "green incentives" directly to farmers' digital wallets, making the cost of sustainable clearing lower than the cost of a fine.

🛠️ Tech Stack
Data: NASA FIRMS (Fire Information for Resource Management System) API.

Backend: [e.g., Node.js / Python FastAPI] for high-concurrency rental transactions.

AI/ML: [e.g., TensorFlow/Scikit-learn] for hotspot prediction and route optimization.

Frontend:HTML,CSS and Javascript designed for low-bandwidth rural connectivity.

📈 Impact Goals
Reduce AQI Spikes: Lower PM2.5 levels by decreasing fire counts by 30% in target districts within year one.

Farmer Profitability: Turn stubble into a resource (biomass) rather than a waste product.

Soil Health: Prevent the loss of essential soil nutrients caused by high-temperature burning.
