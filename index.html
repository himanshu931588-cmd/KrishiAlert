Our Mission: Solving the Stubble Burning Crisis

The Problem Statement

Post-harvest stubble burning causes severe toxic SMOG and air pollution crises across North India. Annually, farmers resort to burning residue because expensive CRM machinery (like Happy Seeders/Balers) and clearing labour are incredibly difficult to rent, locate, and afford during the very short 15-day harvest window.

How KishanSeva Solves It

KishanSeva is a unified digital ecosystem. We leverage NASA Satellite API data and AI to calculate high-risk burning hotspots, instantly route Govt. Subsidies directly to farmers, and provide hyper-local Machinery and Labour bazaars to securely rent required clearing resources before any fires are lit.
